Arabic English Notes
Classroom Vocab
طَيَّب (ṭayyib) good, nice
جَمِيعًا (jamīʿan) (adv.) all together
ما مَعْنًى (mā maʿnan) what meaning, what does it mean?
أُسْتَاذ/أُسْتَاذَة (ʾustāḏ/ʾustāḏa) teacher; professor; title of courtesy commonly given to individuals of high education or learning
مُمْتَاز (mumtāz) excellent; distinguished; privileged
نَعَمْ (naʿam) (adv.) yes
لَا (lā) (particle) no.
وَاجِب (wājib) homework
يَعْنِي (yaʿnī) it means, signifies
كَيْفَ نَقُول ___ بِالعَرَبِيَّة? (kayfa naqūl ___ biālʿarabīyah?) How do we say ___ in Arabic?
فَتْح (fatḥ) fatha vowel sign (short “a”) (هَ)
كَسْرَة (kasrah) kasra vowel sign (short “i”) (هِ)
ضَمَّة (ḍammah) damma vowel sign (short “u”) (هُ)
شَدّة (šadda) shadda. sign of emphasis, diacritic marking a long consonant (هُ)
ألف خنجرية (alif ḵanjariyya) dagger alif, short vertical stroke on top of a letter indicating long “a” sound (هٰ)
تَنْوِين (tanwīn) nunation (هٌ هٍ هً) grammatical indefinite case endings
ألف مقصورَة (alif maqṣūra) Arabic letter alef maksura
تَاء مَرْبُوطَة (tāʾ marbūṭa) ة =variant of the letter ت‎ (tāʾ) used at the end of words
همزة (hamza) ء =letter representing glottal stop
نِسْبَة (nisba) “attribution” - type of adjective used with place names
إِضَافَة (iḍāfah) Idafa construction, noun + genitive
صَحِيح (ṣaḥīḥ) true
صَحّ (ṣaḥḥ) (Hijazi Arabic) right; correct
خَطَأ (ḵaṭaʾ) wrong, incorrect, mistaken
نَصْب (naṣb) accusative case or subjective mood
مَرْفُوع (marfūʿ) nominative case or indicative mood literally, “raised up”.
اَلْمَجْرُورُ (al-maǧrūr) genitive case
مُذَكَّر (muḏakkar) masculine form
مُؤَنَّث (muʾannaṯ) feminine form
جَمْع (jamʿ) plural word
تَشْكِيل (taškīl) diacritics, vowelization
حَرَكَة (ḥaraka) vowel (Arabic)
الجَذْر (al-jaḏr) root (as in root letter)
الوَزْن (wazn) noun or verb “shape”, pattern or form, transfix
جُمْلَة (jumla) sentence (grammar)
اِسْمِيَّة (ismīyah) nominal
الجملة الاسمية (jumla ismīyah) the nominal sentence
